Dr. Santosh Kondekar

Dr. Santosh Kondekar is a Pediatrician in Dadar West, Mumbai and has an experience of 23 years in this field. Dr. Santosh Kondekar practices at Shushrusha Citizens Co-Operative Hospital in Dadar West, Mumbai. He completed MBBS from King Edward Memorial Hospital and Seth Gordhandas Sunderdas Medical College in 1997, MD - Pediatrics from King Edward Memorial Hospital and Seth Gordhandas Sunderdas Medical College in 2002 and DNB from DNB board, New Delhi in 2003.

Dr. Gaurav Gupta

Dr. Gaurav Gupta is an eminent Cardiothoracic & Vascular surgeon, who has a vast experience of two decades, in all complex Cardiovascular and Thoracic procedures. These include Surgical treatment of Adult Heart Disease, Paediatric(Congenital)Heart Disease, Beating Heart surgery, Arterial Revascularization, Complex Re-Operations, Valve Replacement and Repairs, Surgery of Aorta and Arch, Decortication, Mediastinoscopy.
